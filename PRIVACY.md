# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Discord bot **Peedro** ("the Bot") collects, uses, and stores information.

## 1. Data Collected

The Bot may collect and store the following data:

- Discord User IDs
- Discord Server (Guild) IDs
- Channel IDs related to ticket creation
- Messages sent within ticket channels
- Ticket metadata (status, creation time, assigned staff)

The Bot does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

## 2. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used exclusively to:

- Operate and manage the ticket system
- Allow staff members to respond to tickets
- Maintain ticket history and internal logs
- Improve performance, stability, and functionality

## 3. Data Storage

- Data is stored using PostgreSQL and Redis.
- Data is not shared with third parties.
- Reasonable security measures are applied to protect stored data.

## 4. Data Retention

- Ticket-related data may be stored indefinitely unless manually deleted.
- Users may request deletion of their data by contacting the Bot owner.

## 5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

- Request information about what data is stored
- Request deletion of their data

Requests can be made through the official support server.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot implies acceptance of any changes.
